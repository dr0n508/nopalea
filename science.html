What does Nopalea do?

Nopalea is a breakthrough dietary supplement that promotes a state of wellness by supporting the normal and essential anti-inflammatory function performed by the immune system.

Nopalea contains nutrients called Bioflavonoids that are found in the fruit of the Nopal Cactus (Prickly Pear). Bioflavonoids (a powerful class of antioxidants) work to neutralize the body's inner toxins, called free radicals. They do this by helping the body reduce the free radicals attacking your healthy cells, which allows more essential nutrients to reach each cell and neutralize the attack.

Bioflavonoids combat free radicalsthat are causing unnecessary inflammation in your body, restoring balance and reducing inflammation.
